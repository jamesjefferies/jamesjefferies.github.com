Configuring C3p0 in Spring

Apr 22nd, 2008

Tricky, the username and password have to be in the properties bundle as follows:

<bean id=”dataSource” class=”com.mchange.v2.c3p0.ComboPooledDataSource” destroy-method=”close”> <property name=”driverClass”><value>com.mysql.jdbc.Driver</value></property> <property name=”jdbcUrl”><value>${db-connection-url}</value></property> <property name=”properties”> <props> <prop key=”user”>${db-connection-username}</prop> <prop key=”password”>${db-connection-password}</prop> <prop key=”c3p0.timeout”>25200</prop> <prop key=”c3p0.idle_test_period”>14400</prop> <prop key=”c3p0.max_size”>50</prop> </props> </property>

</bean>
